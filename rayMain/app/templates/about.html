About Us

Who We Are?

Ray Advertising, a leading performance-driven marketing agency, specializes in pay-per-call, affiliate network, and lead generation services. Mr. Ripon Kumar, The CEO and founder, started the visionary journey of this company in 2019. Its head office is situated in Redding, California, with a supporting office in Bogra, Bangladesh. Through a vast network of publishers and our in-house media buying team, led by the CEO, we empower businesses to connect with real time customers in generating high-quality leads and targeted traffic.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to be a trusted partner for businesses struggling to grow through innovative performance marketing solutions. By implementing advanced business analytics and strategic affiliate network partnerships between advertisers and publishers, we help businesses connect with high-intent leads and calls that drive sustainable growth for our clients. 

Our Vision

We envision becoming the number one trusted destination for performance marketing and aim to set new standards of excellence in pay-per-call technology, affiliate network partnership, and lead generation tactics. We are heading up to leverage a symbiotic network ecosystem where advertisers can achieve ready-to-convert customers consistently and publishers gain earning potential through 24/7 ethically aligned solutions.
